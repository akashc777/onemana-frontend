OneCamp product update

Hi,

Quick update from me on OneCamp.

We shipped three features I have been building toward for a while. Together they close a loop that most workspaces miss: talk in a call, remember what was decided, remind you when something slips, and automate the repetitive stuff.

1. AI Nudges

You say “I’ll have it done by Thursday” in a call or chat. Thursday passes. Nobody wrote it down, including you.

Nudges fix that. OneCamp already extracts commitments from your conversations. Nudges are the push layer: overdue tasks and stale open questions show up on the bell in your top nav, with a one-line reminder and a link straight to the item. No extra email. No nagging if you already handled it.

2. Meeting Assistant

Every good call has decisions and action items that evaporate the moment it ends.

OneCamp now puts a quiet assistant in the room. It joins your video calls automatically, shows live captions while you talk, and when the call ends it writes a recap (summary, decisions, action items) and posts it right in the channel or DM where the call happened. Then it feeds that into workspace memory, so nudges can pick up the commitments you made out loud.

3. Workflows

Welcome messages. Keyword auto-replies. Turn a message into a task. Light moderation.

You can build these in Settings → Workflows without wiring up Zapier or running a separate bot. A workflow is just a saved rule on top of what OneCamp already does, and it can never do something you couldn’t do by hand.

How to get it

  1. Update your instance to the latest OneCamp release (same process as your last upgrade).
  2. Ask your admin to enable the new features in settings:
    • AI Nudges (requires AI enabled)
    • Meeting transcription and recap
    • Workflows
  3. That’s it. No new servers, no third-party services.
